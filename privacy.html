1. Scope of This Policy

This Privacy Policy applies to our website, mobile games, software applications, interactive products, and other digital services operated by Shukr6 Technoholic unless a separate privacy policy is provided for a specific product.

Our products may use third-party service providers for advertising, analytics, crash reporting, hosting, or other operational purposes. These providers may collect data in accordance with their own policies.

We are committed to protecting children’s privacy. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 except as allowed by applicable law.
